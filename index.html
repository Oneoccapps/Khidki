INTRODUCTION

How it Works!

Khidki Cricket brings the thrill of cricket strategy to your fingertips. Predict runs, wickets, and boundaries in each phase of the match, use special boosts strategically, and compete against friends to prove your cricket knowledge. The smaller your prediction window, the higher your potential rewards!


Know Your Rules

PREDICT THE GAME

Make strategic predictions for each phase of the match. Choose your "Khidki" (prediction range) for runs scored, predict wickets that will fall, and boundaries that will be hit. Smaller Khidkis carry higher risk but offer greater rewards!

DEPLOY SPECIALS

Boost your points with strategic "Specials" like DYS (Double Your Score), WF (Wickets Free), NN (No Negative), GP (Get Points), and ANY2. Each special can only be used once per match, so choose wisely to maximize your advantage!

CLIMB THE RANKINGS

Earn points based on the accuracy of your predictions. Get bonus points for predicting all three events correctly in a phase and for maintaining positive points throughout. Outpredict your opponents to rise to the top of the leaderboard!
